About

My name is Rob Arkell. I am a full stack web developer based in Waterloo, Canada, and I have a Bachelor of Arts degree from Wilfrid Laurier University.

I have previous experience working in civil engineering, manufacturing, operations management, and have also taught English in South Korea. In my free time I also enjoy camping, gaming, travelling, and studying history. These experiences have taught me a lot about constructing well-planned out, functional systems with a global audience in mind, lessons which I strive to integrate into my development work.
